While Impact Schools only make up 3% of New York City's public and charter schools, they are not equally distributed across all 5 boroughs. Queens has 34% of all impact schools, but these makes up only 5% of all public and charter schools in Queens. Staten Island, on the other hand, has only 12% of impact schools but these make up 8% of its public and charter schools. The Bronx, Brooklyn and Manhattan's impact schools make up 1%, 2% and 3% of their public and charter schools respectively.
